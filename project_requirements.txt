# IMPORTANT DATA MATCHING ISSUE

This is the biggest real-world challenge.

* different country names
* different city spellings
* different years

You MUST normalize:

* country codes (ISO-3)
* dates
* city names

This is actually GOOD for your portfolio because it shows real data engineering.
